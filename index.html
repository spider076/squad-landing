Questions & Answers

Answers to your most common questions about our AI agent platform.

1. How do I get started with your AI agents?

Getting started is simple. Create an account, choose the type of agent you need (e.g. business development or marketing), set your preferences, and let the agent start working for you instantly.

2. What kind of tasks can the AI agents handle?

Our agents can perform lead generation, web scraping for business contacts, email writing, social media content creation, scheduling posts, and more. Each agent is specialized for a particular workflow.

3. Can I customize the agent's behavior?

  • Yes! You can define specific keywords, target industries, platforms to post on, and even tone/style preferences for emails or social posts.

  • Advanced users can even set custom workflows and use API hooks for tighter integrations.

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption, do not store sensitive credentials, and all data collection is GDPR-compliant. Your privacy is our top priority.

Yes, our plans are flexible. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard — no questions asked.
